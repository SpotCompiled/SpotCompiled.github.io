About SpotCompiled

SpotCompiled provides compiled Spotify IPAs with various enhancements, including ad-free experiences, unlimited skips, and more. The main projects include SpotC++, SpotveeC, and SpotilifeC, designed to be easily sideloaded onto your device.

Adding to Sideloading Apps

AltStore / SideStore

You can add our repository for automatic updates. Click here to add the repo automatically, or follow the steps below:

  1. In AltStore/SideStore, navigate to the "Browse" tab and tap "Sources" in the top corner.
  2. Tap the "+" button and enter the source URL: https://spotc-repo.yodaluca.dev/AltStore Repo.json
  3. The "SpotC" apps will now appear in your browse tab for easy installation and updates.

Scarlet / Others

Add our repo to Scarlet for automatic updates:

  1. In Scarlet, press and hold the download icon in the top right.
  2. Select "Manage Repos" and then "Add Repo".
  3. Enter the URL: https://spotc-repo.yodaluca.dev/Scarlet Repo.json
  4. SpotC apps will now be available to install and update from within the app.
